Subject: Request for Consideration: Ethical Banking Alignment with Islamic Principles

Assalamu Alaikum [Mosque Name/Leadership Title],

I hope this message finds you in good health and high spirits. I am writing as a concerned member of the Muslim community to bring to your attention an important matter regarding ethical banking practices and their alignment with Islamic principles.

It has come to our attention that some financial institutions, including Barclays Bank, have business relationships and investments that may conflict with Islamic values and our community's stance on social justice, particularly regarding the ongoing situation in Gaza and Palestine.

Islamic Principles and Ethical Banking:
As Muslims, we are guided by principles that emphasize justice (Adl), avoiding harm (La Darar wa la Dirar), and supporting the oppressed. The Quran teaches us: "O you who believe! Stand out firmly for justice, as witnesses to Allah, even if it be against yourselves, your parents, and your relatives" (4:135).

Request for Consideration:
We respectfully request that [Mosque Name] consider:

  • Reviewing current banking relationships to ensure alignment with Islamic ethical principles
  • Exploring alternative banking options that better reflect our community values
  • If currently banking with Barclays, considering divestment as a form of peaceful advocacy
  • Engaging with the community on this important matter

Alternative Banking Options:
There are several Islamic banks and ethical financial institutions operating in the UK that may better align with our principles, including Islamic Bank of Britain, Al Rayan Bank, and various credit unions.

We understand that financial decisions are complex and require careful consideration. We would be happy to provide additional information or resources to assist in this evaluation process.

We pray that Allah (SWT) guides us all in making decisions that reflect our values and contribute to justice and peace in our world.

Barakallahu feekum,
[Your Name]
[Your Contact Information]
[Date]

Note: This letter is designed to be respectful and informative. Always customize it to reflect your personal views and the specific context of each mosque you're contacting.

Best Practices for Outreach

Before You Contact:

  • • Research the mosque's background and community
  • • Understand their current activities and priorities
  • • Prepare to answer questions about alternatives
  • • Have factual information ready

During Contact:

  • • Be respectful and patient
  • • Listen to their concerns and questions
  • • Provide information, don't pressure
  • • Offer to provide additional resources

Frequently Asked Questions

What if a mosque asks for more information?

Be prepared with factual information about Barclays' investments and alternative banking options. Offer to provide additional resources.

How should I handle negative responses?

Remain respectful and understanding. Thank them for their time and consideration. Every conversation contributes to awareness.

Can I coordinate with other activists?

Yes! Share this website and coordinate efforts to avoid duplicate contacts and maximize impact.

Campaign Guidelines & Ethics

Core Principles:

  • • Respect for all individuals and institutions
  • • Peaceful and constructive dialogue
  • • Factual and honest information sharing
  • • Understanding of different perspectives
  • • Commitment to Islamic values of justice and compassion

What NOT to do:

  • • Use aggressive or confrontational language
  • • Share personal information without permission
  • • Make unrealistic demands or threats
  • • Spread misinformation
  • • Harass or repeatedly contact after refusal
