UK Mosques Divestment Campaign

Calling on UK Mosques to Divest from Barclays Bank

Unity Justice Solidarity

Campaign Overview

Why This Campaign Matters

Barclays has significant financial links to companies manufacturing armaments and military technology used in the previous and ongoing assault and killing of innocent Palestinian civilians, including women and children. Banking with Barclays contradicts Islamic principles of justice and solidarity with the oppressed.

We are calling on UK mosques to review their banking relationships and consider divesting from Barclays in alignment with Islamic values of justice and solidarity.

Campaign Goals

  • Raise awareness about Barclays' financial connections
  • Encourage ethical banking alternatives
  • Support Palestinian solidarity through action
  • Unite UK Muslim communities in ethical finance

Draft Letter Template

Subject: Request for Ethical Banking Review - Divestment from Barclays Bank


Assalamu Alaikum,


Dear Respected Leadership of [Mosque Name],


I hope this letter finds you in good health and high spirits. I am writing to you as a concerned member of the Muslim community regarding an important matter that affects our collective commitment to Islamic principles of justice and ethical conduct.


I would like to bring to your attention concerns regarding Barclays Bank's financial connections that may conflict with Islamic values. Barclays has significant financial links to companies manufacturing armaments and military technology used in the previous and ongoing assault and killing of innocent Palestinian civilians, including women and children. Banking with Barclays contradicts Islamic principles of justice and solidarity with the oppressed.


As Muslims, we are called to uphold justice and stand with the oppressed. The Quran reminds us: "O you who believe! Stand out firmly for justice, as witnesses to Allah, even if it be against yourselves, your parents, and your relatives" (4:135).


I respectfully request that [Mosque Name] consider:

  • Reviewing your current banking relationships with Barclays Bank
  • Exploring ethical banking alternatives that align with Islamic values
  • Making a public commitment to divest from institutions that contradict our principles
  • Educating our community about ethical financial choices

There are several ethical banking alternatives available, including Islamic banks and building societies that do not have these concerning financial connections.


I understand that such decisions require careful consideration and consultation. I am happy to provide additional information or resources to support this important discussion within your leadership team.


Thank you for your time and consideration of this matter. May Allah guide us all in making decisions that reflect our commitment to justice and our solidarity with the oppressed.


Barakallahu feek,

[Your Name]
