UK Mosques Divestment Campaign

Encouraging UK mosques to divest from Barclays Bank due to concerns about Gaza

Campaign Overview

Objective

To encourage UK mosques currently banking with Barclays to consider alternative banking options due to concerns about the bank's financial ties and policies related to the situation in Gaza.

Approach

  • Respectful, informative outreach
  • Providing alternative banking options
  • Supporting community decision-making
  • Maintaining interfaith dialogue

Draft Letter Template

Subject: Request for Consideration of Banking Relationship Review

Assalamu Alaikum and peace be upon you,

I hope this letter finds you and your community in good health and high spirits. I am writing to you as a concerned member of the Muslim community in the UK.

I am reaching out regarding your mosque's current banking arrangements, specifically if you are currently banking with Barclays Bank. Recent reports and investigations have highlighted Barclays' financial relationships and investments that some community members find concerning in relation to the ongoing situation in Gaza and Palestine.

Several financial institutions and investment firms have been scrutinized for their connections to companies involved in activities that may contribute to the humanitarian crisis in Gaza. Many individuals and organizations are choosing to align their financial practices with their ethical values by considering alternative banking options.

I would like to respectfully suggest that your mosque consider:

  • Reviewing your current banking relationships
  • Exploring ethical banking alternatives such as Islamic banks or cooperative banks
  • Considering institutions that align more closely with Islamic values and community concerns

Some alternative banking options to consider include:

  • Al Rayan Bank (Islamic banking)
  • The Co-operative Bank (ethical banking)
  • Triodos Bank (sustainable banking)
  • Local credit unions

I understand that changing banking relationships is a significant decision that requires careful consideration of practical factors including services, costs, and convenience. I am not asking for an immediate decision, but rather encouraging thoughtful consideration of how financial choices can reflect our community's values.

If you would like to discuss this matter further or if you need any additional information about alternative banking options, I would be happy to provide resources or facilitate connections with financial advisors familiar with Islamic banking principles.

Thank you for your time and consideration. May Allah guide us all in making decisions that benefit our community and reflect our values.

Barakallahu feeki and with utmost respect,

Campaign Best Practices

  • Always approach with respect and understanding
  • Provide factual information without pressure
  • Offer practical alternatives and support
  • Follow up appropriately but not excessively
  • Respect their decision regardless of outcome
  • Keep detailed records for effective coordination
