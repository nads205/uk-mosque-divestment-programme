UK Mosques Divestment Campaign

Encouraging UK mosques to divest from Barclays Bank due to their financial links to companies manufacturing weapons used against Palestinian civilians

Why This Campaign Matters

The Issue

Barclays has significant financial links to companies manufacturing armaments and military technology used in the previous and ongoing assault and killing of innocent Palestinian civilians, including women and children.

Islamic Principles

Banking with Barclays contradicts Islamic principles of justice and solidarity with the oppressed. Our faith calls us to stand against injustice in all its forms.

Community Action

Together, we can encourage mosques to choose ethical banking alternatives that align with our values and contribute to justice.

Dear Imam [Name] and Mosque Committee,

As-salaam alaikum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh,

I hope this message finds you and the congregation in good health and strong faith. I am writing as a concerned member of our Muslim community regarding an important matter that requires our collective attention and action.

I have recently learned that Barclays Bank has significant financial links to companies manufacturing armaments and military technology used in the previous and ongoing assault and killing of innocent Palestinian civilians, including women and children. Banking with Barclays contradicts Islamic principles of justice and solidarity with the oppressed.

As Muslims, we are called to uphold justice (Adl) and stand with the oppressed (Mustadafin). The Quran clearly states in Surah An-Nisa (4:135): "O you who believe! Stand out firmly for justice, as witnesses to Allah, even if it be against yourselves, your parents, and your relatives."

Our Prophet (peace be upon him) also taught us that "whoever among you sees a wrong action, let him change it with his hand; if he cannot, then with his tongue; if he cannot, then with his heart - and that is the weakest of faith." (Muslim)

I understand that changing banking arrangements can be complex, but I believe our mosque has a moral obligation to ensure that our financial partnerships align with our Islamic values. There are ethical banking alternatives available that do not have these problematic investments.

I respectfully request that the mosque committee:

  • Review the mosque's current banking arrangements with Barclays (if applicable)
  • Consider divesting from Barclays Bank in favor of more ethical alternatives
  • Communicate this decision to the congregation to raise awareness
  • Encourage other local mosques to take similar action

I am happy to assist in researching alternative banking options and would welcome the opportunity to discuss this matter further with the committee.

May Allah guide us all in making decisions that uphold justice and reflect the true values of Islam.

Barakallahu feekum,
